## Dough method

1. Mix everything but oil
2. Let rest for 10 minutes after mixing, then drizzle in the oil
3. Bulk fermentation
4. Proof
5. Sauce and shape

## Cooking

1. 230C bake with base sauce for 10min
2. Add toppings and bake 10-15minutes more
